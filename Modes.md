## Ludicrous Speed

  | Settings Flag     | Value                 |
  | ---:              | :---                  |
  | **Mode**          | Open                  |
  | **Goal**          | All Dungeons          |
  | **Dungeon Items** | Standard* (See Notes) |
  | **Swords**        | Randomized            |
  | **Notes**         | <ul><li>All non-dungeon and non-important items have been removed from the item pool. So that means no heart containers, no heart pieces, no rupees, no bombs, etc.... The compasses have also been removed, but not the maps. Keys and maps remain in the dungeons, they are not shuffled. This means one extra item in each dungeon. (For chest tracker purposes, set compasses to wild/shuffled)</li><br><li>For all other items, they have been multiplied by 4. So 4 sets of Boots, 8 Gloves, 16 Swords, etc.... The compasses are removed to make room for all of the extra items, as it comes out to 216. Most progressive items are replaced with green 20s after you get to capacity (ie: the 5th sword). Extra Mitts do not turn into green 20s. Extra Lanterns turn into blue rupees. Quarter Magic is also available</li><br><li>To make up for the resource issue, the following changes are in: Tree pulls are always bombs (1, 4, and 8); Bush crabs drop 20s (and then a full magic pot); The stun pack is always 20s; The prize packs are completely random.</li></ul>  |
